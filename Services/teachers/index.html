Providing comprehensive curricula focusing on psychological, skill-based, ethical, and sexual development of students aimed at:

Helping students identify their strengths and work on improving them

Developing students' psychological and social personality

Teaching students how to express their emotions and thoughts appropriately

Enabling students to build relationships and choose their friends wisely

Instilling ethical values in students that reflect in their relationships with family and friends

Encouraging students to emulate the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) in moral virtues

Equipping students with essential personal skills to make them more distinguished and interactive with their surroundings

Teaching students the fundamentals of business management, such as administration, marketing, and sales

Raising students' awareness about sexual health and helping them understand their physical and psychological changes

Educating students on the sexual challenges they face and how to deal with them
